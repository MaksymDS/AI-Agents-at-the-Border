# Contributing

Thank you for helping improve a practical guide for border administrations.
This is an editorial repository: accuracy, attribution and operational value
matter more than volume.

## Before you open an issue or PR

1. Search existing issues and the current manuscript.
2. State the affected chapter, appendix, figure or paragraph.
3. Provide a stable source link and publication date for factual claims.
4. Label the evidence: primary administration/regulator, peer-reviewed,
   practice-reported, or commentary.
5. Declare an employer, vendor, client or funding relationship that could
   shape the claim.

## What makes a good contribution

- A correction that is precise and source-backed.
- A customs, border or trade case with a clear task, control design, result,
  limitation and attribution boundary.
- A regulatory update with jurisdiction, legal status and effective date.
- A reader or accessibility improvement that preserves the book's visual
  grammar.

Do not submit confidential operational data, personal data, unverified
performance figures, or promotional copy. A vendor deployment is not an
administration outcome unless the source says so.

## Pull requests

Keep each PR focused. Update the relevant endnote and `research/sources.bib`
with the manuscript change. Run `make audit-publication`, `make check-bib`
and `make check-notes` before requesting review. Editorial review may rewrite,
defer or decline an otherwise valid contribution to retain a coherent book.

Public discussion is deliberately limited to issues and PRs. Editorial feedback
that does not need a public record can be sent through the private form at
[era-society.ae](https://www.era-society.ae).
