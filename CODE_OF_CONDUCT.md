# Code of Conduct

Be specific, civil and evidence-led. Challenge claims, not people. Do not
share confidential, personal, operationally sensitive or procurement-sensitive
information. Harassment, discriminatory language, promotional spam and attempts
to influence editorial decisions through undisclosed commercial interests are
not acceptable.

Report concerns privately through the contact form at
[era-society.ae](https://era-society.ae). Maintainers may edit, close or remove
content that does not meet these standards.
